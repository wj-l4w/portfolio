Project Description

The capstone of my diploma studies, I decided to team with my friends to do what we have long aspired to do: Develop a video game. It is a simple 2D multiplayer battle royale game set in a world that wil be "resetting" due to an impending catastrophe, and survivors need to fight for the last safety pod or be erased from existence.

Skills learnt

Within three months, our team learnt Unity 2019 from scratch, graphics were free 2D sprites procured from itch.io, while the multiplayer aspect was handled by Mirror Networking and Noble Connect for P2P lobbies with Relay and Punchthrough.

Areas to improve

With a strict deadline we barely managed to complete the game in time and there were many polishing bugs that still needed to be ironed out, especially on the UI and multiplayer aspects. Some QoL improvements such as enemy HP bars, back to menu button, audio settings etc could help enhance player experience.
